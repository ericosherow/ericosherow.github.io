Discover The Wishing Pool
The Wishing Pool, an interactive installation, is inspired by mixed reality movements.
Touching upon tropes & philosophies present in Romantic and Japanese Aesthetics, notably the concepts of sublimity and animism,
Wishing Pool is intended to excite a disscusion on the confluence of nature, art, and technology.
